# Audience Signals

> What we know about our target audience from research
> Last updated: 2026-03-02

## Target Audience Profile

**Primary:** Coaches, consultants, and thought leaders who run webinars
**Secondary:** Anyone with valuable content stuck in Zoom/recording folders

## Pain Points (Confirmed Through Research)

1. **The Zoom Graveyard** — they record webinars, upload to a folder, and nothing happens to that content. It's wasted.

2. **Time scarcity** — "I don't have time to repurpose it myself." They're coaches, not content marketers.

3. **DIY frustration** — AI tools (Castmagic, Descript) require too much manual work to get good output. They start, don't finish.

4. **Invisible between webinars** — Between live events, they go quiet. Email list doesn't grow. Audience forgets them.

5. **One-and-done webinar ROI** — Put hours into a webinar, one live showing, then nothing. Poor ROI on prep time.

## How They Describe Their Problem (Their Words)

- "My content just disappears after the webinar"
- "I don't have time to edit video clips"
- "I know I should be posting more but I don't have the bandwidth"
- "I spent 10 hours on that webinar and maybe 40 people watched it live"
- "I've tried using AI tools but they don't get my voice"

## What They Want

- Someone to handle it completely (DFY, not DIY)
- Their voice preserved (not generic AI output)
- Results they can see (new subscribers, engagement)
- Simple delivery (here's your content, here's your link, done)

## What They Fear

- Sounding like AI / losing their authentic voice
- Paying for content they can't use
- Complexity (having to manage another system)
- Committing to a service and not seeing ROI

## Buying Triggers

- Just finished a successful webinar
- About to launch a new program and need content
- Frustrated that their email list isn't growing
- Watching competitors post consistently and wondering how
