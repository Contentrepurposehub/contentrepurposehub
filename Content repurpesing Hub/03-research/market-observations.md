# Market Observations

> Broader market context — trends, shifts, signals
> Last updated: 2026-03-02

## Market Size and Opportunity

- AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) is taking traffic from Google
- Coaches who rank in AI search get qualified leads on autopilot
- Most coaches have no AI search strategy — big gap
- Webinar content is naturally well-structured for AI citation (Q&A, numbered steps, frameworks)

## The AI Search Shift (Key Differentiator)

We optimize for AI citation, not just Google. This is:
- Underserved — most agencies don't mention it
- Durable — AI search is growing fast
- Verifiable — Hive-Rank MCP lets us track it

Most coaches don't even know AI search matters yet. Early mover advantage.

## Content Marketing Landscape for Coaches

- LinkedIn is the dominant channel for coaches and consultants
- Short-form video (YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels) growing
- Email list still the most valuable owned asset
- Twitter/X audience is smaller but higher engagement for B2B

## What's Happening in Our Niche

- "Zoom fatigue" post-2023 has coaches doing fewer webinars
- But those who do webinars are seeing higher-quality audiences (more committed)
- AI tools proliferating — coaches overwhelmed by choices
- Demand for "someone just do it for me" increasing

## Pricing Observations

- DIY AI tools: $30-100/month
- Freelancer on Upwork: $200-500 per webinar
- Agencies: $800-3,000/month for content repurposing
- Our pricing ($750-1,500) is competitive with agencies, much more than DIY

## Signals to Watch

- AI search engine ranking changes
- New competitors entering "DFY webinar repurposing" space
- Platform algorithm changes (LinkedIn, Instagram)
- New AI tools that could change the DIY-vs-service equation
