## 2026-02-12 — Webinar Repurposing Market

**Query:** /last30days run during trend research phase

**Key findings:**
- Coaches increasingly asking "how do I repurpose my webinar without doing all the work myself"
- Pain point: content gets created but never extracted or distributed
- AI tools (Castmagic, Descript) seen as good but require too much manual work
- "Zoom graveyard" is a recognized problem people talk about

**Implications:**
- "Zoom folder" language resonates — use it in copy
- Position against DIY AI tools, not just other agencies
- Pain is real and recognized — not creating the need, just capturing it
