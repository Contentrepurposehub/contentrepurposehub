## Landing Page Standards (Growth and Scale)

MUST include:
1. VideoBackground component (local video clip, not YouTube)
2. AnimatedCard component (staggered fade-up on scroll)
3. AnimatedCounter component (count-up on scroll)
4. Two local video clips extracted via yt-dlp + ffmpeg
5. Lead capture in 3 positions
6. 15 sections minimum
7. Brand-matched colors from client's actual website
8. ZERO YouTube/external links on any CTA

## Email Flywheel (All Tiers)

Every lead MUST go to both:
1. Our Google Sheet (always)
2. Client's email platform (Beehiiv, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign)

If email platform env vars aren't set, leads only go to our sheet. Broken flywheel.
