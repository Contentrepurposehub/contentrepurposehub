# Agency Positioning

> Last updated: 2026-03-02

## What We Do

Webinar repurposing service for coaches and thought leaders. We take a single 60-minute webinar and produce 14+ content pieces: blog post, newsletter, landing page copy, 10+ LinkedIn posts, lead magnet concept, and more.

## Target Customer

Coaches, consultants, and thought leaders who:
- Run webinars (live or recorded)
- Have valuable content stuck in Zoom folders
- Don't have time to repurpose it themselves
- Want an email list that grows between live events

## Core Differentiator

Full-funnel focus, not just clips. Competitors produce video clips. We produce the SEO layer (blog), the email layer (newsletter + welcome sequence), the social layer (LinkedIn), and the capture layer (landing page + lead magnet). All optimized for AI search citation, not just Google.

## Positioning Statement

"You spent 10 hours preparing that webinar. Then it vanished into a Zoom folder. We turn one webinar into a month of content that grows your email list — without you lifting a finger."

## What We Are Not

- Not a video clipping tool (Chopcast, Parmonic)
- Not an AI-only tool (ContentWorks AI, Castmagic)
- Not a generalist content agency (Komet Media, Videodeck)
- Not just social clips — we build the whole funnel

- 1 webinar = 14+ content pieces
