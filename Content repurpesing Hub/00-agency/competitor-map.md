## Top Competitors by Threat Level

### HIGH Threat

**repurposemywebinar.com**
- Appears in 3 of 7 priority queries (best service, agency, DFY)
- Listicle-style blog post ranking for multiple queries simultaneously
- Closest competitor in positioning
- Weakness: Research shows negative reviews around limited output scope

### MEDIUM Threat

**kometmedia.com**
- Unlimited webinar repurposing model
- Different niche focus (not coach-specific)
- Appears in: webinar content repurposing agency, DFY webinar repurposing

**goldcast.io**
- Enterprise/B2B focus
- AI-driven repurposing
- Not coach-focused — different ICP

**willowpaule.com**
- DFY content creation specifically for coaches
- Closest to our ICP (coaches)
- Appears in: content repurposing for coaches

### LOW Threat (Tools, Not Services)

**Chopcast** — self-serve + some DFY, abandoned in 2025
**Parmonic** — AI clip generation
**Riverside** — all-in-one recording platform
**ContentWorks AI** — AI-only tool for coaches
**Castmagic** — audio/video to text tool
**contentfries.com** — DIY tool
**quso.ai** — video repurposing tool

## Key Positioning Gaps We Can Own

1. **"Content repurposing for coaches"** — top results are small blogs, not services. Strong opportunity.
2. **"DFY webinar repurposing"** — only 1 comparison article in top 5. Our DIY vs Service post competes.
3. **Full-funnel angle** — competitors focus on clips/social. Nobody owns the full-funnel (blog + email + social + landing page) story.
4. **AI search optimization** — we explicitly optimize for AI citation. Competitors mostly ignore this.
