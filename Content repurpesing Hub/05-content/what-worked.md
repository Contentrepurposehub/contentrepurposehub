## Known Content Principles (Not Yet Validated With Data)

These are bets we're making based on research and SEO best practices:

### Structural Bets

1. **Comparison posts** — competitor alternative queries tend to rank faster (lower competition, clearer intent)
2. **Original research** — /blog/webinar-repurposing-benchmarks-2026 targets AI citation specifically with citable data
3. **Definitional posts** — AI search loves to cite "what is X" posts for simple queries
4. **FAQ schema** — captures "people also ask" without ranking in the traditional 10 blue links

### Voice Bets

1. **Specific numbers** — "1 webinar = 14 pieces" beats "many content pieces"
2. **Anti-AI writing** — posts that don't sound AI-generated stand out in a sea of AI content
3. **Practitioner perspective** — "Here's what actually works" vs theory
